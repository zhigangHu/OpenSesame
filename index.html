Overview and Motivation

The data is not always the boring number. Sometimes you can earn the money by mining them. So we called this “Data value Digging”. Many people are very interesting in investing stock market, for example Dow 29, Nasdaq, S&P 500. But most of the investors have no idea what kind of stock I should buy, what time is the best time to sell. If they don’t make clear of these questions they will lost money instead earning money.

Project Objectives

In this project we plan to build a data visualization web site. By visualizing the stock market data to help people who want to invest in stock market to make decision, what kind of stock they should buy, what time is the best time to buy and what time is the best time to sell, what the invest risk, what is the invest profit
